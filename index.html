Other Work

I’ve co-run MAP (Minorities and Philosophy) at Hopkins for several years with Rima Hussein. I am particularly interestest in increasing accessibility to graduate school to undergraduates of diverse backgrounds, and have co-run an annual workshop for students to help them apply, as well as running mentoring during the application season.

Here is a handout and slides for the workshop we run - I try to keep it updated annually. Please get in touch if you have any additional suggestions, or would like advice on applying to graduate school.

I was fortunate to be elected to the bargaining committee that negotiated the first union contract for graduate students at Johns Hopkins University, with our union TRU-UE.
